Features

  • Combats Stubble Burning: Eliminates the harmful practice of burning stubble, preventing air pollution and its associated health problems.
  • Nutrient-Rich Mulch Creation: Chops and mulches stubble into a valuable resource that retains moisture, suppresses weeds, and reduces soil erosion.
  • Natural Fertilizer: Over time, decomposed stubble enriches the soil with essential nutrients like nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium, reducing reliance on chemical fertilizers.
  • Enhanced Soil Health: Improves soil structure and organic matter content, fostering beneficial microbes and regulating soil temperature for healthier crops.
  • Sustainable Practices: Promotes sustainable agriculture by eliminating burning and encouraging the use of natural fertilizers.
  • Cost-Effective: Offers an affordable solution for farmers compared to the recurring costs of chemical fertilizers.
